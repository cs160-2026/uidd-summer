Hello! My name is Vivian!

I am a third-year PhD student in the EECS Department at UC Berkeley, in the [Hybrid Ecologies Lab](https://hybrid-ecologies.org/), advised by Prof. Eric Paulos. I hold a joint Master's degree in Design Engineering from Brown University and Rhode Island School of Design (RISD). Previously, I was a visiting research student at the MIT HCI Engineering Group, advised by Prof. Stefanie Mueller and Visiting Prof. Megan Hofmann, and an undergraduate HCI researcher at the National Taiwan University HCI Lab, advised by Prof. Lung-Pan Cheng.

I am deeply inspired by, and constantly in awe of, how nature makes things and solves problems. In my free time, I enjoy drawing and watching animal documentaries.
